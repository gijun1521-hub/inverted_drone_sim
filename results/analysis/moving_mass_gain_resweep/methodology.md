# Methodology

Seven full-duration mirrored LOITER/Pitch/stick-release scenarios were evaluated deterministically with the merged PR #22 Pitch controller fixed. Stage 1: 0.000..0.200 m/Nm by 0.025 plus 0.1325. Stage 2: best +/-0.025 by 0.0025. Stage 3: best +/-0.005 by 0.0005, extending only an upper boundary and never above 0.300 without authorization. Raw aggregate score uses the pre-existing pitch-retune scenario weights and group aggregation; no near-equivalent tie-break is used.
